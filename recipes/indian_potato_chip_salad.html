Indian Potato Chip Salad

Description

This Indian street food often features crispy fried things topped with all kinds of amazing garnishes. It's by far one of the most delicious foods in the world.

Ingredients

  • 12 cups kettle-cooked potato chips
  • 0.5 cup thinly sliced jalapeno pepper
  • 0.5 cup thinly sliced red bell pepper
  • 0.5 cup thinly sliced red onion
  • 0.5 cup thinly sliced green onion
  • 1 cup sliced cooked purple potatoes (Optional)
  • 1 cup chopped fresh mint leaves
  • 1 cup chopped fresh cilantro leaves
  • 2 medium limes, halved
